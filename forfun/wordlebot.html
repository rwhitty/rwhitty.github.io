Wordle Solver

Directions:

- This solver is designed to be used alongside a game of Wordle.
- To solicit the solver's guess, click the "bot guess" button.
- Input that guess (or any other) into your Wordle game.
- Click "submit guess", and click the letters of your submitted guess to describe the pattern from Wordle.
- Click "submit pattern", and repeat! (Don't use this to cheat!)
